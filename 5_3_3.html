12 questions | Numeric response format | Formulas for frequency, period, and wave speed
Formula 1: Wave Equation
Formula: $v = f\lambda$

Variables: $v$ = wave speed (m/s), $f$ = frequency (Hz), $\lambda$ = wavelength (m)
3.1
A sound wave in air has a frequency of 440 Hz and a wavelength of 0.75 m. Determine the speed of the sound wave.
Answer: _____ m/s
3.2
A longitudinal wave traveling through water has a wavelength of 2.0 m and a speed of 1500 m/s. Determine the frequency of the wave.
Answer: _____ Hz
3.3
Determine the wavelength of a sound wave with a frequency of 250 Hz traveling through air at a speed of 340 m/s.
Answer: _____ m
3.4
An ultrasonic wave travels through tissue at a speed of 1540 m/s. If the wavelength is 0.154 mm, determine the frequency of the ultrasonic wave.
Answer: _____ Hz (or _____ MHz)
Formula 2: Frequency-Period Relationship
Formula: $f = \frac{1}{T}$ or $T = \frac{1}{f}$

Variables: $f$ = frequency (Hz), $T$ = period (seconds)
3.5
A sound wave has a period of 0.01 s. Determine the frequency of this sound wave.
Answer: _____ Hz
3.6
A longitudinal wave has a frequency of 200 Hz. Determine the period of the wave.
Answer: _____ s
3.7
Determine the frequency of a wave that completes one full oscillation every 0.002 s.
Answer: _____ Hz
3.8
A low-frequency wave used in medical imaging has a period of 1.0 × 10⁻⁶ s. Determine its frequency.
Answer: _____ Hz (or _____ MHz)
Formula 3: Distance from Echo Time
Formula: $d = vt$

Variables: $d$ = distance (m), $v$ = speed (m/s), $t$ = time (s)
3.9
A sound wave travels through air at a speed of 343 m/s. How far does the sound travel in 5.0 seconds?
Answer: _____ m
3.10
A person shouts across a canyon. The echo returns after 3.0 seconds. The speed of sound in air is 340 m/s. Determine the distance to the canyon wall. (Note: the sound travels to the wall and back.)
Answer: _____ m
3.11
Determine how long it takes for a sound wave traveling at 1500 m/s through water to travel a distance of 3000 m.
Answer: _____ s
3.12
A sonar pulse is sent from a boat, and the echo returns from the ocean floor after 0.8 seconds. The speed of sound in seawater is 1540 m/s. Determine the depth of the ocean at that location. (Note: sound travels down and back up.)
Answer: _____ m
