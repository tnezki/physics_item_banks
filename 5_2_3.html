Formula 1: Frequency and Period Relationship
Formula: $f = \frac{1}{T}$ or $T = \frac{1}{f}$

Variables: $f$ = frequency (Hz or cycles/s), $T$ = period (seconds)
3.1
A vibrating tuning fork completes one full oscillation in 0.002 seconds. Determine the frequency of the tuning fork.
Answer: _____ Hz
3.2
Determine the period of a wave if its frequency is 60 Hz.
Answer: _____ s
3.3
A musical instrument produces a note with a frequency of 440 Hz (the musical note A4). Calculate the period of this note's wave.
Answer: _____ s
3.4
A pendulum swings back and forth, completing 2 full cycles every 4 seconds. What is the frequency of the pendulum?
Answer: _____ Hz
Formula 2: Wave Equation
Formula: $v = f\lambda$

Variables: $v$ = wave speed (m/s), $f$ = frequency (Hz), $\lambda$ = wavelength (m)
3.5
A transverse wave has a frequency of 50 Hz and a wavelength of 2 meters. Calculate the wave speed.
Answer: _____ m/s
3.6
A wave travels at 20 m/s with a frequency of 10 Hz. Determine the wavelength.
Answer: _____ m
3.7
A water wave has a wavelength of 4 meters and travels at 8 m/s. Find the frequency of the wave.
Answer: _____ Hz
3.8
A sound wave in water travels at 1500 m/s. If the frequency is 300 Hz, what is the wavelength?
Answer: _____ m
Formula 3: Wave Speed (Alternative Form)
Formula: $v = \frac{\lambda}{T}$

Variables: $v$ = wave speed (m/s), $\lambda$ = wavelength (m), $T$ = period (s)
3.9
A wave has a wavelength of 8 meters and a period of 2 seconds. Calculate the wave speed.
Answer: _____ m/s
3.10
A transverse wave on a string travels at 12 m/s. If the wavelength is 3 meters, find the period of the wave.
Answer: _____ s
3.11
A ripple on a pond has a period of 1.5 seconds and a wavelength of 0.75 meters. Calculate the speed of the ripple.
Answer: _____ m/s
3.12
Find the wave speed of a wave with a wavelength of 0.5 meters and a period of 0.1 seconds.
Answer: _____ m/s
