A. an elastic collision

A. compare momentum before and after

A. make the collision more elastic

18. Two pool balls (each 0.17 kg) collide. Ball A at 4.0 m/s strikes ball B at rest. After collision, ball A moves at 0.50 m/s and ball B at 3.5 m/s. Calculate kinetic energy before and after, and determine if the collision was elastic.
