16. A sled (40 kg with rider) slides down a frictionless hill from 25 m to 8.0 m above ground. Calculate potential energy at both heights, find the change, and determine the speed at 8.0 m if starting from rest. (Use $g = 9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$)
