Formula 1: Period and Frequency Relationship
Formula: $T = \frac{1}{f}$ or $f = \frac{1}{T}$

Variables: $T$ = period (seconds), $f$ = frequency (Hz or cycles/s)

Formula 2: Hooke's Law
Formula: $F = -kx$ or $|F| = kx$ (magnitude)

Variables: $F$ = restoring force (N), $k$ = spring constant (N/m), $x$ = displacement (m)

Formula 4: Simple Pendulum Period
Formula: $T = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}$

Variables: $T$ = period (s), $L$ = length (m), $g$ = gravitational acceleration (9.8 m/s²)
