17. A home uses an average of 25 kWh per day. Calculate the average power consumption rate. Answer in watts.
18. A gaming console draws 8.5 amperes at 12 volts. If a gamer plays for 3.0 hours per day for an entire year (365 days), calculate the annual energy consumption. Answer in kilowatt-hours.
19. A cordless vacuum has a 2.5 ampere-hour battery at 24 volts. Determine how many minutes it can operate if it draws a constant 6.0 amperes. Answer in minutes.
20. An office building uses 800 kWh of energy per day. If the average voltage is 240 volts and the building operates loads for an equivalent of 16 hours per day, determine the average current draw. Answer in amperes.
21. A power bank stores 10,000 mAh at 3.7 volts. Calculate how many times it can fully charge a phone with a 3000 mAh battery at 3.7 volts, assuming 80% efficiency. Answer as a whole number.
