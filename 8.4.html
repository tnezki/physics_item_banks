4. A circuit has ($R_1 = 30$Ω parallel with $R_2 = 60$Ω) in series with $R_3 = 15$Ω, powered by 105V. Calculate: (a) parallel equivalent, (b) total resistance, (c) total current. Answer: total current in amperes.
5. From Q4: with 3.0A total current and $R_3 = 15$Ω, calculate voltage drop across $R_3$. Answer in volts.
6. From Q4-Q5: Total voltage 105V minus 45V across $R_3$ leaves voltage for parallel section. Calculate current through 30Ω branch. Answer in amperes.

8. Using Q7 result and a 48V battery, calculate total current. Answer in amperes.
9. From Q7-Q8: Calculate voltage drop across $R_1 = 10$Ω with current from Q8. Answer in volts.

12. From Q11: Calculate power dissipated in the 6.0Ω series resistor carrying 2.0A. Answer in watts.
13. A circuit has three parallel branches: 10Ω, 20Ω, and 30Ω, powered by 60V. Calculate equivalent resistance (use reciprocal formula). Answer in ohms.

17. Using Q16 result and 40V battery, calculate total current. Answer in amperes.
18. From Q16-Q17: Calculate voltage drop across $R_1 = 5$Ω. Answer in volts.
