17. A 0.058 kg tennis ball approaches a racket at 30 m/s and rebounds at 40 m/s in the opposite direction. The racket (mass 0.35 kg) was initially at rest. Calculate the ball's change in momentum, then find the racket's final velocity.
18. In a physics lab, a 1.2 kg glider at 2.5 m/s collides with a 0.80 kg glider moving at 1.5 m/s in the opposite direction. After collision, the 1.2 kg glider moves at 0.50 m/s in its original direction. Calculate the initial total momentum, then find the 0.80 kg glider's final velocity.
