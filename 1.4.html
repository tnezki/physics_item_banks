Bank 3 – Single-Step Calculations (Numeric Response)

1. Determine the x-component of a vector with a magnitude of 20 units directed at an angle of 0° from the positive x-axis.
2. A vector has a magnitude of 10 units and is directed at 60° above the positive x-axis. Calculate the x-component of the vector.
3. Find the y-component of a vector with a magnitude of 15 units directed at 90° from the positive x-axis.
4. A displacement vector has a magnitude of 12 units and makes an angle of 30° with the positive x-axis. Determine the y-component of the displacement.
5. Calculate the x-component of a vector with magnitude 25 units directed at 45° from the positive x-axis.
6. A force of 18 units acts at an angle of 60° from the positive x-axis. Determine the y-component of the force.
7. Find the magnitude of a vector that has an x-component of 6 units and a y-component of 8 units.
8. A vector has components of x = 9 units and y = 12 units. Determine the magnitude of the vector.
9. Calculate the magnitude of a vector whose x-component is 5 units and y-component is 5 units.
10. A displacement has components x = 3 units and y = 4 units. Find the magnitude of the displacement.
11. Determine the direction (angle from the positive x-axis) of a vector that has an x-component of 10 units and a y-component of 0 units.
12. A vector has components x = 0 units and y = 7 units. Calculate the direction of the vector relative to the positive x-axis.
13. Find the direction of a vector with an x-component of 4 units and a y-component of 4 units.
14. A vector has components x = 8 units and y = 8 units. Determine the direction of the vector from the positive x-axis.
15. Calculate the y-component of a vector with magnitude 40 units directed at 30° from the positive x-axis.
16. A vector is directed at 45° from the positive x-axis and has a magnitude of 14 units. Determine both the x- and y-components of the vector.
17. Find the x-component of a vector with magnitude 50 units directed at 60° from the positive x-axis.
18. A vector has an x-component of 12 units and a y-component of 5 units. Determine the magnitude of the vector.
19. Calculate the direction of a vector with x = 6 units and y = 6 units.
20. A vector has a magnitude of 20 units and is directed at 30° from the positive x-axis. Determine the y-component of the vector.

Bank 4 – Multi-Step Vector Problems (Numeric Response)

1. A force applied to a crate has a magnitude of 20 N and acts at 30° above the horizontal. Determine the magnitude of the force using its x- and y-components.
2. Find the direction (from the positive x-axis) of a displacement vector whose components are 12 m east and 5 m north.
3. A tugboat pulls a barge with a force of 40 N at an angle of 45° relative to the shoreline. Calculate the direction of the force after resolving it into components.
4. Determine the magnitude of a velocity vector that has components 9 m/s east and 12 m/s north.
5. A drone moves with a velocity of 50 m/s at an angle of 60° above the horizontal. Find the direction of the motion after resolving the velocity into components.
6. Calculate the magnitude of a displacement whose components are 6 m east and 8 m north.
7. A hiker walks with a displacement of 30 m at 30° north of east. Determine the direction of the displacement after finding its components.
8. Find the direction of a wind velocity vector with components 10 m/s east and 10 m/s north.
9. A force of 25 N acts on a sign at an angle of 45° from the horizontal. Determine the magnitude of the force using its components.
10. Determine the magnitude of a displacement vector with components 5 m east and 12 m north.
11. A boat crosses a river with a velocity of 14 m/s directed at 45° relative to the riverbank. Calculate the direction after resolving the velocity into components.
12. Find the direction of a displacement vector whose components are 8 m east and 6 m north.
13. A force of 10 N is applied at an angle of 30° above the horizontal. Determine the magnitude of the force using its x- and y-components.
14. Calculate the direction of a displacement formed by walking 3 m east and 4 m north.
15. A rescue helicopter moves 26 m at an angle of 60° above the horizontal. Determine the magnitude of the displacement using its components.
16. Determine the direction of a force that has an x-component of 15 N and no y-component.
17. A skier travels with a velocity of 35 m/s at 45° downhill relative to the horizontal. Find the direction of the velocity after computing its components.
18. Calculate the magnitude of a vector whose components are 20 units in the x-direction and 15 units in the y-direction.
19. A package is pulled along the ground with a force of 18 N at 30° above the horizontal. Determine the direction after resolving the force into components.
20. Find the direction of a displacement vector whose components are 7 m east and 7 m north.
